Time to be the light

You are a solitary light wandering around an unknown place, full of mysterious objects you must explore. You must pay careful attention to their shadows and forms, so to gain knowledge about the world you are in. Move the light with the keyboard arrows. Use W and S to move the light up and down. Use U , to upgrade the light's power when you've traveled enough. For small viewports use the small controller at the bottom of the screen. Remember carefully, this world is very dark. It needs you to be the light.
